Privacy Policy for Sudoku World

Last updated: October 2025

Sudoku World uses your device's camera only to recognize Sudoku puzzles for solving purposes. No images or data are stored or shared.

Data We Collect

  • Anonymous usage data for performance improvement.
  • AdMob advertising identifiers for showing relevant ads.
  • RevenueCat purchase data for processing in-app purchases.

How We Use Data

All data is used solely to enhance gameplay, display ads, and process purchases. No data is sold or shared with third parties.
